## Key Rules

1. **Alina cannot act on secrets she hasn't discovered yet.** Her deductions must follow the reveal chain.
2. **Steers withholds strategically.** She knows more than she tells Alina at every stage. She reveals information when it serves the investigation — or when she's forced to.
3. **Parker gives pieces, not the whole picture.** "Survival." is all she offers for chapters. She lets others assemble the truth.
4. **The Executive knows the institutional side** but may not know Parker's personal experience (her believing she was Evie, her relationship with Evie's father).
5. **James knows nothing after Ch 1.** He is incapacitated. Do not have anyone reference information "from James" that he couldn't have shared before the inspection.
