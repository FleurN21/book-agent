# Parker [LAST NAME]

## Role
Complex antagonist / victim / mirror to Alina

## Basics
- **Age:** ~19-20 (wiped at 16, three years ago)
- **Appearance:** Small for her frame. Bird-like. Fragile. Too thin — malnourished. Matted hair (would need cutting but they won't let her near scissors). Sharp grey eyes. W tattoo on cheek from 3 years ago, still visible.
- **First impression:** Alina expected a monster. Instead: ordinary. Normal. Small. Then the eyes — grey and sharp. Then the smile.

## Personality
- **Core trait:** Precision. Everything she does is intentional. Nothing is wasted.
- **Flaw:** Has come to see violence as a tool, not a last resort.
- **Fear:** Being experimented on again. Being trapped.
- **Want:** To be wiped — to start over with a clean slate and have a chance at her own life
- **Need:** To be seen as a person, not a copy or an experiment

## Voice
- **Speech patterns:** Default is silence. When she speaks: one word or one devastating sentence. States facts as though describing weather.
- **Sample lines:** "Survival." / "No you don't." / "Because she wouldn't look like that if you did."
- **What she won't say:** The full truth directly. Gives pieces. Lets others assemble.

## Relationship to Memory Technology
- **Role:** Was wiped at 16. Became a test subject for memory transfer. Has all of Evie's memories.
- **Institutional allegiance:** None. She is a victim of every institution.
- **Wipe status:** Supposedly wiped 3 years ago. Actually: taken by the Institute, given Evie's memories. Never truly wiped (or wiped and then filled with someone else's memories).
- **What she knows:** Everything. She knows about the transfer technology, about Evie's father, about the Institute's plans. She is the proof that it works.

## Mystery Role
- **Connection:** She killed Evie. She attacked James. She has Evie's memories. She is the central mystery.
- **What she's hiding:** The tea shop memory (Evie's memory), the truth about what she is, who made her, why she killed Evie.
- **Key scenes:** Ch 1 (inspection/smile), Ch 10 (says "Survival"), Ch 12 (sees through the bluff), Ch 20 (mentions Project Maya), the final Parker scene (reveals everything).
- **What Alina believes vs. true:** Monster → human. Murderer → victim. Stranger → someone carrying Evie inside her.

## The Full Truth (SPOILER)
Parker was a 16-year-old convicted of a Grade A crime [original crime TBD — Steers says it wasn't evil]. She was wiped. The Institute took her instead of sending her to reintegration. Evie's father transferred all of Evie's memories into her. For a time, she believed she WAS Evie. Then the real Evie visited the lab. Parker saw her and realised something was wrong — these weren't her memories. She was a copy. Evie's father forced more memories into her. Let her know she was a copy. Kept her awake for experiments. Parker came to hate Evie — or tried to. She killed Evie believing she could replace her and live the life she dreamed of. She was wrong. She let herself get caught because she wanted to be wiped — to start over for real this time.

## Relationships
- **Alina:** Recognises her from Evie's memories. A complicated pull — Alina is the person "Evie" loved most.
- **Evie:** Carries her memories. Was her, then wasn't. Killed her.
- **Evie's father:** Her torturer. The man who made her and kept her captive.
- **James:** She attacked him deliberately to protect the tea shop memory. She knew what she was doing.
- **Steers:** Steers wants to investigate her. Parker knows about Project Maya — knows Steers' secret.

## Arc
- Introduced as a monster. Slowly revealed as a victim. Dies saving Alina — choosing someone else over herself for the first time.
