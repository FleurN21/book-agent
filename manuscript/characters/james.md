# James [LAST NAME]

## Role
Ally / love interest / catalyst (his destruction drives Alina's guilt)

## Basics
- **Age:** Similar to Alina (early-to-mid 20s)
- **Appearance:** Handsome, full of life. Easy charisma. Physical presence that fills a room. After the incident: pale, frail, mad eyes, muttering.
- **First impression:** The kind of person you can't help but like — and can't help resenting for it.

## Personality
- **Core trait:** Genuine kindness. He's the best of them — talented AND good.
- **Flaw:** Perhaps too accommodating. He does what Alina asks even when he shouldn't (the inspection).
- **Fear:** [Pre-incident: TBD. Post-incident: trapped in someone else's memories]
- **Want:** To help Alina. To do good work.
- **Need:** Someone to protect him from his own kindness.

## Voice
- **Speech patterns (pre-incident):** Warm, easy, reassuring. Doesn't need many words.
- **Sample line:** "Are you sure you want to be here for this?"
- **Post-incident:** "Get her out of my head." Incoherent screaming. Muttering.

## Relationship to Memory Technology
- **Role:** Watcher — rising star. Works for Amanda Steers.
- **Institutional allegiance:** Justice Department (Steers' protege)
- **Wipe status:** Never wiped. Mentally destroyed by Parker's attack during the inspection.
- **What he knows:** Before the incident, standard watcher knowledge. After: broken.

## Mystery Role
- **Connection:** He was the watcher during Parker's inspection. She attacked him, leaving him mad. He asked the fatal question: "Why did you kill her?"
- **What he's hiding:** Nothing intentionally. He pulled strings to be the watcher on Evie's case as a favour to Alina.
- **Key scenes:** Ch 1 (inspection), Ch 4 (Alina sees his madness), referenced throughout as motivation.
- **What Alina believes vs. true:** Alina believes his destruction is her fault (she asked him to do it). This is largely true, and the guilt is deserved.

## Relationships
- **Alina:** Former academy rival → best friend → unresolved romantic tension. He always believed in her. He understood her jealousy. He came through for her after Evie's death.
- **Steers:** Boss and mentor. She valued him as the best watcher she had.
- **Evie:** Knew her through Alina. Never close friends, but knew of her.

## Arc
- Enters as the golden boy. Destroyed in Chapter 1. Exists for the rest of the novel as a haunting presence — the cost of the conspiracy made personal. His mad eyes are what Alina sees every time she closes hers.
