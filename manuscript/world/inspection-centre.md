# Inspection Centre

## Overview
Where memory inspections (watches) take place. Repurposed from an old police station — the old world literally converted into the new. This is where the inciting incident occurs (Ch 1).

## Sensory Details
- Sight: Mix of old architecture (police station bones) and new technology (pods, monitors, control room)
- Sound: Deathly quiet. Any sound feels like a violation — unoiled hinges, shuffling papers. Like talking over a prayer.
- Atmosphere shift: Calm and routine before the inspection. Chaos when things go wrong — alarms, shouting, machines going haywire.

## Story Function
- Ch 1: The inspection of Parker. James enters the pod. Everything goes wrong.
- The control room behind the two-way mirror: where Alina watches
- The hallway outside: where James is carried out unconscious, loaded into a black van

## Spatial Details
- Control room with two-way mirror (Alina can see the inspection chamber, but not vice versa — though James knows where she's sitting)
- Two pods in the inspection chamber
- Monitoring equipment, recording setup
- A door that slams as operators leave
- Outside: where the black van pulls up (not an ambulance)

## Consistency Notes
- Alina can see a phrase referencing the Eden Institute on the wall — hints at their philosophy
- James glances up at the mirror knowing where Alina sits
- Parker knows about the emergency exit button near the feet of the pod
- The session was done late in the evening (not fully official — James pulled strings)
